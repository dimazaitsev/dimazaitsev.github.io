References for Performance Evaluation of Networks via Reenterable Colored Petri Nets

A model for networking technology having unchanged structure for any modeled network. Actual network topology is given as a parameter (place marking). Ideal for Model-Driven Design of networks, grids, and clouds. Recently applied for verification of cryptocurrency.
